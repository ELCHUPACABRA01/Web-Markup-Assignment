Public transportation in Amsterdam is efficient, well-connected, and an excellent way to explore the city and its surrounding areas. The most convenient way for tourists to access these transports is to buy an OV-chipkaart (a smart card for public transport). It can be bought in stores. An OV-chipkaart can be loaded with credit in OV-chipkaart machines, Shops or with a bank card. Visit: OV-Chipkaart.nl - OV-Chipkaart.nl for detailed information.

Download the 9292 app for detailed schedule and route of trams and buses. Simply type your starting location and destination and the app displays the trum and bus number and route.

For android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=nl.negentwee. For IOS id556557690.

Download the NS app for train and metro schedule, route and platform number. Similar to the 9292 app, type the starting station and destination station and the app displays the details of the train going to that station.

For android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=nl.ns.android.activity. For IOS:https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ns-travel-planner/id370362301 . The available public transports in Amsterdam are as follows:
  • 1.Trams:Amsterdam's extensive tram network covers most parts of the city. Trams are a convenient way to get around the city center and reach popular attraction.
  • 2. Buses:Amsterdam's bus network complements the tram system and extends to neighborhoods not covered by trams. Buses are operated by GVB (Gemeentelijk Vervoerbedrijf), the city's public transport company.
  • 3. Metro:The Amsterdam Metro system has four lines (M1, M2, M3, and M4) that connect the city center with suburbs and areas like Amsterdam Arena and RAI Convention Centre. It's a fast way to travel longer distances within the city.
  • 4. TrainsAmsterdam has several train stations, with Amsterdam Centraal being the main hub. Trains are a great way to explore other Dutch cities and regions, including day trips to places like Haarlem, Utrecht, and Rotterdam.
  • 5. Ferries:Amsterdam is known for its canals, and several free ferries operate across the IJ River, connecting the city center to the north. These ferries provide access to attractions like Eye Film Museum and NDSM Wharf..
